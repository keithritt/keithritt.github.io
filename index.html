UNCG Wrestling Reunion

Friday October 4th - Saturday October 5th

Greensboro, NC

Join us this October to celebrate the UNCG wrestling program and meet up with fellow alumns.

The reunion weekend will coinside with the UNCG Homecoming and the induction of Joe Lowe into the Hall of Fame.




Event Schedule:



Friday: 4:00 PM

Meet at Old Town for food and drinks. Afterwards, we can hop around to other places, potentially: Corner Bar, Jake's Billiards, Suds & Duds.


Saturday: 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony in the Cone Ballroom on campus. Joe Lowe, along with 6 others will be honored. All UNCG wrestling alumni are invited, tickets are $50, brunch is included. Reach out to Danielle Sandridge at dwsandridge@uncg.edu if you have any questions.
RSVP Deadline: September 24th.


Saturday: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM

Official UNCG Alumni Reunion in Flemming Gym. Our conference championship banners will be put back up. The athletic director Brian Mackin will give a speech and fellow alumn will have an opportunity to speak. Drinks and Snacks will be provided, friends and families are welcome.


Saturday: 4:00 PM

Again, meet at Old Town for food and drinks. Afterwards, we can hop around downtown Greensboro, potentially: Boxcar, South End Brewery, Natty Greene's, Stumble Stilkins, One Thirteen Brewhouse
