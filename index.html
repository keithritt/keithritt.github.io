UNCG Wrestling Reunion

Friday October 4th - Saturday October 5th

Greensboro, NC

Join us this October to celebrate the UNCG wrestling program and meet up with fellow alumns (40 and counting).

The reunion weekend will coinside with the UNCG Homecoming and the induction of Joe Lowe into the Hall of Fame.

If you haven't already, please fill out the RSVP Form Please help spread the word, we have a lot of alumni that we have not been able to get in touch with.


Event Schedule:



Friday: 4:00 PM

Meet at Old Town for food and drinks. Afterwards, we can hop around to other places, potentially: Corner Bar, Jake's Billiards, Suds & Duds.


Saturday: 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony in the Cone Ballroom on campus. Joe Lowe, along with 6 others will be honored. All UNCG wrestling alumni are invited, tickets are $50, brunch is included. Reach out to Danielle Sandridge at dwsandridge@uncg.edu if you have any questions.
RSVP Deadline: September 24th.


Saturday: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM

Official UNCG Alumni Reunion in Flemming Gym. Our conference championship banners will be put back up. The athletic director Brian Mackin will give a speech and fellow alumn will have an opportunity to speak. Drinks and Snacks will be provided, friends and families are welcome.


Saturday: 4:00 PM

Again, meet at Old Town for food and drinks. Afterwards, we can hop around downtown Greensboro, potentially: Boxcar, South End Brewery, Natty Greene's, Stumble Stilkins, One Thirteen Brewhouse.

Email Invitation:

On August 1st, UNCG sent a message to all of the available wrestling alumni emails it had. If you did not get the email, you can read it below. If you would like to be added to future alumni emails, please contact Chase Blake (cbblake@uncg.edu).
UNCG Wrestling Alumni,

It is with great pleasure that I write to you about the various opportunities we have set—thanks to the efforts of some fellow program alumni—during this year's Homecoming Weekend at UNCG (October 4-5). As some of you may have heard, another well-deserving Spartan wrestler, Joe Lowe, is being inducted into the UNCG Athletics Hall of Fame. His induction ceremony will be held on campus inside Cone Ballroom that Saturday, October 5, at 9:30 am.

Click here for the official release on his selection.

Official invitations to attend will be mailed out to teammates of Joe soon, but you can also register for the ceremony by contacting Danielle Sandridge, UNCG Athletics Event Manager, at dwsandridge@uncg.edu. Tickets are $50 each, which include a brunch buffet. I have attached the invitation and reply card to this email so that you can return to Danielle.

Alumni Reunion Event
Additionally, as part of the Homecoming festivities, we would like to invite you to an official Wrestling Alumni Reunion inside Fleming Gymnasium on Saturday, October 5 at 2:00pm. Here we would like to welcome you and your families with the opportunity to connect with teammates and friends. Drinks and snacks will be provided along with hearing a few words from fellow teammates and UNCG Athletic Director, Brian Mackin.

This opportunity will run officially from 2:00pm - 3:30pm, giving time for you to attend the Hall of Fame ceremony and to participate in many of the other activities happening on campus.

Click here to RSVP for the Alumni Reunion

Of note, the full slate of Homecoming events has not yet been finalized by the University, but once it is, we will be sure to share it in a follow-up email.

Hotel Information
The Sheraton at 3121 W Gate City Blvd is a partner with UNCG Athletics and they have set up a block rate of rooms should you need one for the night/weekend.

Block Name: UNCG Homecoming 2024
Rate: $161 per night (double + king available)
Contact: 1 (888) 236-2427


We are all excited about the events happening on campus and hope that you can join us for a weekend back at the G. Also - please be sure to spread the word to those that may be interested in joining - as we may not have everyone's updated contact information.

All the best and Go Spartans!

Chase B. Blake

Associate Athletic Director for Development
